The Kimosabe Guarantee: We never see your files. All processing happens 100% inside your web browser. Your data never leaves your computer.

Kimosabe Tools uses modern web technologies to perform "Client-Side Processing." This means when you select a PDF or an Image, your browser does the work. Our servers are used only to host the website's code; they do not have the capability to store, view, or receive your personal files.

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to monitor website traffic and improve user experience. This service collects anonymized information such as:

This data does not identify you personally and is used solely for statistical purposes.
